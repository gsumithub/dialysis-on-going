Our Story & Philosophy

DialysisOnGo was launched with a single vision: to give dialysis patients the freedom to travel. Finding a trusted clinic while traveling shouldn't be a source of stress. We connect patients with accredited, high-quality dialysis clinics across India, ensuring clinical continuity and safety wherever you go.

For our clinical partners (B2B), we provide a streamlined booking pipeline that reduces empty slots, matches viral-isolated patient groups to proper machines, and helps clinics manage travel schedules effortlessly.
